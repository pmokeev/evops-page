In the course of the work, various algorithms were investigated. Our evaluation list includes the following algorithms: CAPE, PEAC, DDFPP, Storm-irit, Region Growing (PCLRG) from PCL library, Connected Components from PCL library (PCLCC). The performance of the algorithms was evaluated using our benchmark on various metrics.

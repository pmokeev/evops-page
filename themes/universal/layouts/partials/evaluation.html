In the course of the work, various algorithms were evaluated on our data. Our evaluation list includes the following algorithms: CAPE, PEAC, modified region growing, automatic extraction of multi-scale structures, Region Growing (PCL library implementation), Connected Components (PCL library implementation). The performance of the algorithms was evaluated using evops on metrics, such as, precision, recall, F-score, intersection over union (IoU), DICE.
